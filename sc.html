Score Calculation

GAA = Avg of Best 9 out of 10 weekly assignments BPTA = Avg of 4 BPT scores QZ1 = Quiz 1 Score (0 if not attempted) OPE = Online Proctored Exam Score F = Final Exam Score Total Score T = 0.10 × GAA + 0.2 × Qz1 + 0.3 × OPE + 0.3 × F + 0.1 × BPTA
